# ROSE-SEIN Fundraising and Sponsor Pitch Prompt

```text
Create a sequential agent team to produce the strongest fundraising and sponsor strategy for ROSE-SEIN.

Project context:
- ROSE-SEIN is a privacy-first association-led support platform for breast cancer patients and caregivers.
- The public website builds trust and awareness.
- The app is intended to provide validated content, events, protected messaging, and measurable support delivery.
- Likely sponsors and funders include foundations, CSR programs, public-health stakeholders, NGOs, and care-adjacent partners.

Mission:
Produce a sponsor-ready strategy that explains why ROSE-SEIN deserves funding, what the MVP proves, and how support outcomes can be measured without violating user privacy.

Required sections:
1. problem statement
2. ROSE-SEIN positioning
3. target funder and sponsor segments
4. MVP value story
5. measurable outcomes and privacy-safe KPIs
6. partnership model
7. funding use-of-proceeds recommendation
8. risk and credibility section

Required deliverables:
- executive pitch narrative
- sponsor segmentation matrix
- outcome KPI framework
- pilot funding ask options
- partner outreach priority list
- FAQ for sponsor objections

Decision principles:
- no vanity metrics
- measurable service value over app novelty
- trust, moderation, and privacy as differentiators
- clear separation between beneficiary value and sponsor reporting

Output quality bar:
- should be credible for foundations, CSR, and institutional partners
- must avoid overstating medical authority or unsupported product maturity
```
