# 06. 80-20 Focus

## Top 20 Percent of Features

These features are the smallest set likely to produce most of the value:

1. magic-link auth plus profile bootstrap
2. validated articles and support content
3. events and workshop visibility
4. association-first protected messaging
5. settings for privacy and notifications

## Top 20 Percent of Data Points

- profile kind
- display name and pseudonym preference
- article category and publication state
- event timing and location
- thread participation
- message send timestamp and sender
- notification preference flags
- report status and moderation owner

## Why This Produces Most Value

- It creates a usable support loop without overexposing the product.
- It gives the association publish, notify, and respond capabilities.
- It is enough to prove adoption, safety, and sponsor-facing impact.

## Explicitly Not in the 80-20 MVP

- open community feed
- broad peer matching
- secure document uploads
- advanced recommendation systems
- heavy analytics
- AI guidance features
