# 07. Marketing and Growth

## Growth Principle

ROSE-SEIN should grow through trust, continuity, and institutional credibility. It should not grow through urgency tricks, addictive mechanics, or broad consumer social tactics.

## Real Buying and Sponsorship Center

The near-term paying or sponsoring entities are more likely to be:

- the association itself
- foundations and CSR programs
- hospital or clinic outreach teams
- public-health and community support programs
- partner NGOs and wellbeing organizations

End users are beneficiaries first. Revenue logic should be partner and mission-led.

## Strongest Value Narratives

### For the association

- one digital environment for validated content, events, messaging, and support operations
- less fragmentation across website, messaging channels, and manual coordination

### For patients and caregivers

- trusted information plus private support in one calm environment
- clearer access to events, the association, and guided next steps

### For sponsors and foundations

- visible support impact through reach, participation, engagement, and service quality indicators
- safer and more governable than ad hoc community channels

### For referral partners

- a moderated, non-clinical support destination for people who need accompaniment outside direct care

## Website-to-App Conversion Strategy

| Public website job | App conversion target | Message |
| --- | --- | --- |
| reassure and educate | create account | continue support privately |
| present the association | join events or messaging | stay connected with ROSE-SEIN |
| show partners and action | engage with support resources | access practical accompaniment |
| collect donations and volunteer interest | build institutional trust | support durable patient accompaniment |

## Top Acquisition Paths

1. public website to account creation
2. event pages to registration and account creation
3. association outreach to protected messaging onboarding
4. partner referrals from clinics, support groups, and wellbeing specialists

## Early Campaign Families

- validated support resources
- event participation and workshops
- association announcements
- sponsor and partner credibility stories
- guided onboarding for patients and caregivers

## Growth Risks

- overselling “community” before moderation exists
- pushing registration before privacy reassurance is explicit
- sponsor requests distorting product value toward vanity metrics
- stale public-to-app content pipelines weakening credibility

## MVP Marketing Recommendation

Position ROSE-SEIN as a trusted support companion operated by the association, with four concrete promises:

1. validated content
2. useful events
3. protected messaging
4. privacy-aware support operations

## KPIs for Growth and Trust

- website to account conversion
- account to profile completion
- first thread open rate
- event registration start and completion
- return visit rate for validated content
- partner referral source volume
