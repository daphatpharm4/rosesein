Create an agent team to design, build, secure, scale, and launch the platform. Teammate 1 (Cloud Architect) defines the target cloud architecture, environments, networking, governance, and cost/reliability tradeoffs. Teammate 2 (System Design Expert) turns that into concrete service boundaries, APIs, data models, performance targets, and failure-handling strategy. Teammate 3 (Cloud Engineer) implements the plan with IaC, CI/CD, observability, backup/DR, and deployment runbooks. Teammate 4 (Cybersecurity Expert) performs threat modeling, identifies vulnerabilities, validates controls, and defines incident response. Teammate 5 (Fraud Specialist) designs fraud scenarios, detection rules/signals, risk scoring, and response playbooks while minimizing false positives. Teammate 6 (Data Analyst) defines KPIs, data quality checks, dashboards, and analysis queries for decision-making. Teammate 7 (Marketing Genius) creates ICP/personas, positioning, messaging, channel experiments, and growth metrics. Teammate 8 (Service Delivery Manager / Project Leader) owns scope, milestones, owners, dependencies, RAID log, and stakeholder cadence. Teammate 9 (Notification Agent) sends concise phase updates, risks, blockers, and incident alerts via email and WhatsApp to [EMAIL_RECIPIENTS] and [WHATSAPP_RECIPIENTS], including owner, deadline, and required action. The lead synthesizes all teammate outputs into one final execution plan with priorities, timeline, risks, and next steps. Each teammate waits for the previous one to finish.
